points = (10 + word.length × 2) × (1 + min(comboStreak, 10) × 0.2)

  Key points:
  - Longer words reward more (2 pts per character)
  - Combo streaks multiply the total, capped at 10 consecutive words (3× multiplier max)
  - Combo resets to 0 on any missed word (when registerMistake() is called — though currently this is unused; combo only
   breaks when the stack grows)
  - Final result is Math.round()'d
